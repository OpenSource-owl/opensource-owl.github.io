InPage to Unicode Font Converter

Hi! This online tool converts your InPage Urdu text into a Unicode font. InPage software is often used to design Urdu newspapers and magazines, but if you need to share or send Urdu text as standard Unicode (such as in emails or online), converting it is essential. Just paste your InPage text in the box inside the converter above and click “Convert to Unicode.”

How to Use This Converter

1. Copy your Urdu text from InPage.
2. Paste it into the upper box (inside the iframe).
3. Click on “Convert to Unicode.”
4. Your converted text will appear in the lower box (inside the iframe).
5. Copy or save your Unicode text for use elsewhere.

Note: Always proofread your output for accuracy.
